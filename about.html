About Smaz

Smaz is a next-generation technology brand focused on building smart, connected, and futuristic digital products. Our mission is to merge AI, social, and lifestyle apps into one seamless ecosystem – starting with our first product Lina.

Our Mission

At Smaz, our mission is simple: to create technology that feels human. We build apps that understand your needs, connect your world, and make everyday life smarter and easier.

Our Vision

We envision a future where AI and social apps work together – making communication effortless, entertainment limitless, and productivity natural. Smaz is the platform that powers this future.

Our Story

Founded in 2025, Smaz was born with a single idea – to build a smarter digital experience. From the very beginning, our focus has been on combining AI-powered assistance with social interactions in a way that no app had ever done before.

Our first product, Lina, is proof of this vision. But Smaz is more than one app – it’s a growing ecosystem of technology designed for the future.

Our Core Values

Innovation

We push boundaries by integrating AI, design, and technology into every product we create.

Trust

Privacy, transparency, and user trust are at the heart of everything we build.

Future-Ready

We design apps that evolve and scale for tomorrow’s digital world.
