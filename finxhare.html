Frequently Asked Questions

Is FinXhare free to use?

Yes — the core FinXhare platform is free. Premium features and advanced reports may be added in the future.

When will FinXhare be fully launched?

FinXhare is already live for core features. Additional modules like advanced reports and custom dashboards are in active development.

Can I manage both personal and business finances?

Absolutely — FinXhare is designed to handle personal, business, and custom setups all in one platform.

Is my financial data secure?

Security is a top priority. FinXhare uses secure storage and privacy protocols to protect your data.

Can I export reports and analytics?

Yes — the reporting module will support PDF and CSV exports, along with charts and analytics dashboards (currently under development).
